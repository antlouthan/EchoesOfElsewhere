Echoes of Elsewhere
An Actual Play Podcast that crosses the boundaries between worlds and rules.
What happens when ordinary lives collide with extraordinary realms? In this actual play TTRPG series, modern-day heroes are pulled into a dimension where myth, mystery, and danger reign. If you crave character-driven storytelling, cinematic stakes, and collaborative world-building, this is your call to adventure.


Season 1 - Coming Soon


Game Systems
The heroes in the modern world will play using the Pulp Cthulhu rules by Chaosium. When they find themselves in another world, they'll switch to the Dungeons and Dragons 5th edition rules by WOTC.
